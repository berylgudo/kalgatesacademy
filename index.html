KALGATES ACADEMY FEATURED AMENITIES

Kalagates Academy is not just any other Institution, it is distinctive in its academic excellence coupled with activity vitality.

Music

Improve Self-esteem. Music allows pupils to try something new and develop confidence as they master singing or playing an instrument.

Homescience practicals

Studying home science provides students with excellent career opportunities and also enables them to manage their homes efficiently.

Art and craft projects

Arts and craft activities help instil a sense of achievement and pride in children, boosting theirself-confidence. The opportunity to create whatever a child desires helps foster creativity.

Taekwondo

Develops courtesy and respect, including self-respect. Improves self-control, cooperation, and physical coordination. Teaches how to set and achieve goals.

Skating

Skating for exercise helps kids burn off extra energy, build strong muscles, and maintain a healthy weight.

Swimming classes

Improves fitness. Swimming is a low-impact form of exercise that provides a great alternative to sedentary activities like watching TV and playing computer games. A skill for life and it's fun!

Computer learning

E-learning platforms and applications give pupils new tools to problem-solve and study, which has changed the academic world.

General Learning

The school is not only meant for activities. We also offer general CBC programs and subjects like Mathematics and English which is the main mode of communication in the school

Transportation

Our transport comprises of new and reliable buses and is available to all our pupils on request. As safety and comfort of our children is our priority.

Meals

Enriched breakfast porridge and beverages are provided to our pupils in the morning and afternoon. A well-balanced hot meal for all, including pilau make our delicious lunch.
