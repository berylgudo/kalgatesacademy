The Founder

Kalgates Academy was founded in 2020 January,by Mr kalugo, administrator for more than 20yrs,a teacher for more than 40yrs.
in March 2020 we had to close because of Corona pandemic, we continued building classes with hope that once we got back we would be bigger and greater.
In September when school doors opened, our enrollment moved frm 40kids to 199 kids,
in January 2021 the enrollment was 254 pupils frm baby class to Grade 4. July 2021 we had an enrollment of 376 upto Grade 5. We are doing purely CBC.
